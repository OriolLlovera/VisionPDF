# VisionPDF — OCR for Scanned & Image-Based PDFs

Extract text from scanned PDFs, image-only PDFs, FlipHTML5 exports, photographed documents, and scanned books directly in your browser.

VisionPDF is a free browser-based OCR tool built for PDFs that do NOT contain selectable text.

Unlike traditional PDF readers or converters, VisionPDF can detect when a PDF is actually composed of images/photos and automatically apply OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to recover the text.

---

## Why VisionPDF?

Most online PDF tools only work with:
- selectable PDFs
- digital text layers
- standard exported PDFs

VisionPDF is designed specifically for:
- scanned PDFs
- image-based PDFs
- photographed documents
- FlipHTML5 downloads
- scanned books
- manga/comics scans
- OCR text extraction from images inside PDFs

Everything runs locally in your browser:
- no uploads
- no servers
- no subscriptions
- no limits
- no tracking

# How It Works

VisionPDF automatically detects whether your PDF already contains text.

Workflow:

```text
PDF Upload
    ↓
Text Detection
    ↓
OCR (if needed)
    ↓
Page-by-page extraction
    ↓
Text viewer + export
```

For scanned PDFs:
- pages are rendered as images
- OCR is applied
- extracted text is cleaned and displayed

For standard PDFs:
- text is extracted directly without OCR

# Usage

1. Open VisionPDF
2. Drag & drop a PDF
3. VisionPDF detects whether OCR is required
4. Click “Extract Text”
5. Browse extracted pages
6. Export the results
